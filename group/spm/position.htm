Job Opening  
 

Openings are available right now and applicants will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Positions may vary from postdoctoral research fellow to associate professor.

 

Research areas include:

  • Pattern formation and pattern selection;

  • Self-organized growth phenomena; Interfacial growth; Crystallization;

  • Phase separation in polymer systems;

  • Scanning probe microscopy and scanning electronic microscopy and related techniques

 

Applications should be sent with a full CV, a brief statement of research interest and names of two referees to Prof. Mu Wang, NLSSMS & Dept of Physics, Nanjing University, Nanjing 210093, China.

(Email: muwang@nju.edu.cn)
