General Information

The research interests of this laboratory mainly focus on the mechanism of interfacial growth, nonlinear phenomena in crystallization and self-organized fabrication of nano-structured materials.

With optical microscopies (interference contrast microscopy, interferometry and polar-light microscopy), scanning probe microscopy (AFM & STM) and field-emission scanning electron microscopy, we are studing the process and the mechanism of nucleation, self-organized crystallization and aggregation. Particular interest is addressed to the relations between the interfacial morphology and growth conditions in crystallization; the relations between the stability of the concentration field and the interfacial processes; and the pattern formation and pattern selection in far-from-equilibrium interfacial growth.

王牧研究组近年来利用光学原位观测技术、扫描探针显微术、电子显微术和微区同步辐射X光衍射等手段研究了晶体生长系统和电结晶系统中的若干界面生长形态及其生成机制，研究了界面演变的过程及其动力学规律，揭示了传输限制系统中特有的生长界面附近物理、化学环境的周期改变对生长形态和生长机制的影响。
