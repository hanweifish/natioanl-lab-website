Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
Most recently Wang's group has done following work:
1. Wang and Yan studied on C60, theoretical modeling of the system of two different relaxation processes and then found the correlation between relaxation units is an important factor on the behavior of glass transition. Concluded that the strong correlation between relaxation units leads to a phase-transition-like freezing process, while the weak correlation gives rise to a kinetic freezing process.
2. Zhang built up the first low-temperature polarized electronic Raman scattering system in China . The system can work below 4.2K and use polarized incident light for high-quality single crystals. As a powerful technique, electronic Raman scattering has been widely used to explore superconducting energy gap and its anisotropy, carrier dynamics and spin fluctuations in cuprate superconductors. Some electronic Raman Scattering experiments have been started on high-quality single crystals of LSCO, YBCO and BSCCO cuprate superconductors. He found that dynamical electronic stripes may exist in LSCO system, which suggests an inhomogeneous electronic structure should be taken into account in understanding superconductivity in LSCO.
3. Ding and Wang revealed firstly that the anti-phase boundary (APB) can be considered as a nucleation site for new domain and curved 90 o domain has better mobility. Both of them play an important role in fatigue free behaviors of BLS ferroelectric materials. Wang gave two invited talks at different international conferences about this result.
4. Lu studied the various properties of ferrolectric low dimensional materials, including substitution, fatigue mechanism,size and stress effects etc.. In recent years, SPM was used to investigate the local switching property of ferroelectric.
5. Su and Wang found the curved 90 o domain wall can absorb much more oxygen vacancies than straight domain wall, it is the origin of fatigue free in some BLS ferroelectrics.
6. Li and Wang proposed that the motion of oxygen vacancy pinned domain wall has stronger correlation.
