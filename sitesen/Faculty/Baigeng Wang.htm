Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
My recent interests focus on quantum transport properties in mesoscopic and normal/superconductor hybrid systems by using Keldysh Green's function technique. The basic questions we ask are: how to predict electric current flowing through a nano-device connected to the outside world by metallic electrodes? How does the interaction between electrons have effect on the current-voltage characteristic of a mesoscopic system? How to use the electron spin degree of freedom to design new quantum devices? What is the new law of heat transport in nanostructures? These problems are directly relevant to the future microelectronics technology.
