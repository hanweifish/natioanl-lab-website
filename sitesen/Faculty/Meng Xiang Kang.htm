Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
We synthesized thin film and core-shell structured particles ranging from approximately 3 nm to over 100 nm in size by means of co-sputtering and chemical vapor deposition. Microstructure evolution and mechanical property variation relative to size effects were emphasized in our research.
