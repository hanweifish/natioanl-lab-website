Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
We developed X-ray scattering technique for characterizing surfacial and interfacial microstructures in films, including the magnetic multilayers, semiconducting superlattices and heterostructures, perovskite-like films and heterostructures, etc. The relationship between the surfacial morphology and the transport property in films are recently developed. Rietveld refinements are focus on the microstructural characterized powder diffraction for dopping oxides. Stain distribution and phase transition in perovskite-like oxides, such as SrTiO3.
