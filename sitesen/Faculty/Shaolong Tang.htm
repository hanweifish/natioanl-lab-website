Research area (up to 40 words)

My research centres on the fabrication and characterisation of magnetic materials. The range of topics covered include:
(1) Permanent magnetic materials;
(2) Gaint magnetostrictive materials;
(3) Magnetic recording media.

Description of recent research activities ( up to 200 words)

(1) RE:Fe:B permanent magnet thin films for MagMEMS;
(2) High-pressure synthesis of giant magnetostrictive Pr-Tb-Fe alloys;
(3) Magnetic nanowire and nanoparticle arrays for application in h igh-density perpendicular recording and magnetic sensor.
