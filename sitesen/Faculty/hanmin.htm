Research area

 Cluster physics; Cluster-assembled nanostructures; Electronic and optical properties of nanostructured materials; Dynamical process at nanoscale
Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
Development of new cluster beam technology: a new generation pulse laser ablation type cluster source and magnetron plasma aggregation cluster source have been developed; Novel nanostructure syntheses with directed cluster beam deposition as well as energetic cluster impacting; Gas phase syntheses of arrays of silicon-riched nanoclusters and nanowires and their optical properties; Self-assembling of metal nanoclusters through template guiding and surface plasmon optical coupling and the SPR enhanced optics of the metal nanocluster arrays; Thermal and fluid process at nanoscale: the thermodynamical phase transition of nanoclusters, nanometer scale jetting; Temperature-dependent metal-nonmetal transition of nanocluster arrays.
