Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
•  Fabrication of high quality and low-dimensional ferroelectrics potential for non-volatile storage, such as PZT and Bi-layered perovskite system, including substitution to adjust the electrical properties, controllable growth to prepare thin film with expected structure and character, as well as making ferroelectric nano-structures;
•  Investigation of memory-related phenomena of ferroelectrics, including fatigue mechanism, switching kinetics, size and stress effects;
•  SPM investigation of the domain structure and the local switching properties of ferroelectrics, including films and slices.
