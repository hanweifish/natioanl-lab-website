Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
Recent research focused on macroscopic quantum phenomena and quantum computation with Josephson tunnel junction qubits and superconducting persistent current qubits. Demonstrated macroscopic quantum coherent oscillations with relatively long decoherence time in a Josephson junction. Observed Mach-Zehnder interfermetry of persistent current qubits. Demonstrated long (~ 20 m s) energy relaxation time in Nb persistent current qubits.
