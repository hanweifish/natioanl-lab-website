Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
[1] Solid C 60 has potential application for gas storage because it has octahedral voids in its fcc lattice. Using solid 13 C NMR and dielectric method, it has been studied that the structure, phase transition and glass transition of solid C 60 doped with non- and polarized molecules. [2] The ferromagnetic C 60 polymer has been prepared under the condition of high temprature and high pressure, and its structure and mechanism of ferromagnetism is being studied by solid NMR method. [3] The impurity hydrogen as point defects will unavoidably enter ferroelectric crystal LiNbO 3 during growth of the crystal, and affect on optical preperties. The sites and concentration of the defects inside the crystal are being studied by solid solid 1 H NMR.
