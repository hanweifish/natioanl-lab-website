Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
We are working on the complexity simplification and folding behavior for proteins based on simplified and all-atom protein models. For the complexity simplification, we use different statistical methods to reduce the alphabet of the natural occurring 20 kinds of amino acids into several kinds N<20 to study the physical features of the protein structures and sequences. For the folding, long time simulations based on different protein models, especial the all-atom model are performing to characterize the thermodynamic and kinetic features. Our purpose is to understand the physical features and mechanism of biological functions of proteins.
