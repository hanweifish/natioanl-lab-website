Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
The current research of Prof. Zhu's group is in the fields of nonlinear and guided-wave optics, dielectric superlattices, quantum optics and quantum information, efficient generation of coherent visible and infrared radiation by bulk optic frequency conversion and all-solid-state technique, optical scattering in nonlinear photonic crystals, preparation and characterization of engineering ferroelectric-domain, photoniccrystal, acoustic crystals and man-made band-gap materials, negative-index and left-hand materials, interaction between electron, ion beams and ferroelectric and piezoelectric materials etc.
