Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
Participating in 973 project on nano-materials and nanostructures. Carrying out the project of National Foundation of Natural Science in China on quantum transport in nanostructures. Participating in the key project of National Foundation of Natural Science in China on CMR materials. Supervising more than 10 Ph. D students in research on Condensed Matter Theory. Taking collaboration with several experimental research groups on Condensed Matter Physics. Recently, the main topics of research are: 1) Quantum interference and their effects in nanostructures; 2) Interaction between spins and other excitations in materials; 3) Quantum decoherence and dephasing; 4) Possible mechanisms in nanostructures for processing of quantum information.
