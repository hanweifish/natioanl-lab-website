Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
•  Physics of transition metal oxides (colossal magnetoresistance and phase separation, low-field magnetoresistance enhancement, strongly correlated electron systems, Fermi liquids, spin-dependent physics, multi-ordered heterstructures) .
•  Physics of ferroelectrics and dielectrics (ferroelectric thin films, phase transitions, dynamics of relaxation) .
•  Phase transitions and magnetoelectric coupling in multiferroic systems .
•  Computer simulations on phase transitions in solids, liquids and low-dimensional systems, Monte-Carlo simulations, topics on magnetism, thermodynamics and statistical physics, pattern formations and kinetics of liquid-solid transformations .
•  Physics and application of functional ceramic thin films and heterostructures, and their applications (magnetoresistive, magnetoelectric, ferroelectric, piezoelectric, pyroelectric, electro-optic and nonlinear optics) .
•  Preparation of integrated ferroelectric, electric-optic and nonlinear optics (ferroelectric memory, ferroelectric field effect transistor, micro-electric-mechanics system, wave-guide in films) .
