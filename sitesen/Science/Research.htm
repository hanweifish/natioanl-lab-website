NLSSM: Research

 

Research Aim and Scope

The laboratory has developed its major research interests in investigating the microstructures in various sorts of condensed matter at different levels, revealing the internal relationships between these microstructures and their macro-properties, and developing advanced materials and new devices with expected microstructures and performance by the active and coherent approaches of theoretical modelings, computer simulations and advanced experiments.

The major research areas of the laboratory include but not limited to the physics of microstructured dielectric materials, nano-structured materials and physics, aggregations and pattern formation under non-equilibrium conditions, dynamics of microstructural assembly and modulation, strong correlation effect in solids, phase transitions and related microstructural physics in solids as well.
