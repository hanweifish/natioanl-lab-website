Go to Nanjing University from Nanjing Railway Station

1. Take subway line No.1 to the station Zhujiang Road (the fourth stop), get out of the subway station from the third exit. Walk north to the first cross. Then turn left. Go straight along the Hankou Road for about 100 m. The main gate of Nanjing University is on the right side of the road.

2. Take Bus 22, or 38, or 1, to the stop of Zhujiang Road. Get off the bus and walk backward to the first cross. Then turn left and go straight along the Hankou Road for about 100 m. The main gate of Nanjing University is on the right side of the road.

Go to Nanjing University from Lukou Airport

1.By taxi: the distance is about 35 km, and cost about 150 RMB.

2.Take the shuttle bus to get into the city. Then take taxi or bus to Hankou Road. In any case the taxi fare should be within 15 RMB.
