Description of recent research activities ( up to 200 words)

 

From 1999 to 2005 he was the director of the National Laboratory of Solid State Microstructures of Nanjing University. Right now he is the director of the Nanjing National Laboratory of Microstructures, and also the chairman of the Academic Degree Committee of the Physics Department of Nanjing University. His main research field is the condensed matter theory, in particular the transport theory in nanostructured systems. More than two hundreds papers have been published in the international academic journals, such as 9 in Physical Review Letters and 91 in Physical Review.
