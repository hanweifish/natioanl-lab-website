Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
Dr. Wang's research interests include nonlinear optics, Nanophotonics, and laser science and technology. His recent researches includes 1) all-solid-state laser systems based on nonlinear frequency conversions in optical superlattice, and passively mode-locked lasers, 2) nonlinearities of novel materials and characterizing techniques, 3) novel optical phenomena in defective nonlinear photonic crystals and photonic crystal fibers, such as enhanced dual-localized second-harmonic generations and optical parametric oscillations, 4) electromagnetic properties, underlying physics and potential applications of metamaterials, especially in left-handed materials, and 5) methods in producing optical vertex and its applications, such as in micromanipulation. Since 2000, Dr. Wang has finished one book chapter, published over 70 research papers, and has been awarded over 8 patents.
