Description of recent research activities
( up to 200 words)
My reseach interest is in physical properties of microstructured materials. The materials studied include: (1) Engineered ferroelectric domains (named as dielectric superlattice), which shows excellent nonlinear optical properties and piezoelectric properties which can be used for nonlinear optics, ultrasonic wave excitation and phonon-polariton excitation.; (2) Subwavelength structures in metal films, which exhibits abnormal light transmission and is necessary for plasmonics research (a short term for surface plasmon-based photonics). (3) Left-handed materials. The study is beneficial for understanding the physics of new phenomena such as coupled parametric nonlinear optical process, polariton associated with longitudinal superlattice vibration and enhanced light transmission.
